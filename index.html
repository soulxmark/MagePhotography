Shawarma

Our Shawama dish consisting of meat cut into thin slices, stacked in a cone like shape, and roasted on a slowly turning vertical rotisserie or spit. Originally made with lamb or mutton, it is now also made of chicken, turkey, beef, or veal.


Liempo (Pork Belly)

Inihaw na Liempo marinated in citrus juice, fish sauce, and spices and grilled to juicy perfection. Delicious as an appetizer or main dish!

Burger
